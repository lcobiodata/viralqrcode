Privacy Policy

Privacy Notice

Effective:

This site temporarily creates a unique, anonymous browser signature using device characteristics like current time, timezone, location, and IP address. This signature is used only to generate an ephemeral encryption key. These traits are processed locally, are never stored or transmitted, and are discarded immediately after use.

We store no personal information and do not profile, track, or identify users. The only data saved—locally in your browser—is limited to information you explicitly generate during your session, including:

  • URL query parameters
  • Current Bitcoin block ("Time Anchor")
  • Encrypted metadata generated through interactive features
This encrypted data may include inputs from certain hidden or playful elements ("easter eggs") you encounter.

Some site features are intentionally concealed or triggered by specific interactions. While these may produce or modify encrypted data, no hidden feature ever collects personal information or sends any data to a server. Everything stays on your device.

We believe privacy should be the default. If you have questions or discover something surprising, feel free to contact the site operator.

This notice reflects the current technical design and data-handling behavior of this site.
